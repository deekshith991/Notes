##### **passive elements**:
Elements that can only dissipate o store energy but not generate energy.
E.g: [[Resistor]], Inductor, Capacitor, etc.

##### **Network**:
The interconnection of two or more simple circuit elements forms an electrical network.

##### **Circuit**:
If the network contains at least one closed path, it is also an electric circuit.
> [!Note]
> Every circuit is a network, but not all networks are circuits

##### Conductance:
The conductance is a measure of how well an element will conduct electric current. The unit of conductance is the mho (ohm spelled backward) or reciprocal ohm, with symbol , the inverted omega
